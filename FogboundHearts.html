Fogbound Hearts

By Aleks Filmore

A Queer Gothic Romance of Curses, Obsession, and the Fog That Never Lifts

Read on Amazon

Some secrets are best left buried in the fog.

When Daniel Hartley inherits a decaying seaside mansion in the forgotten town of Eldermist, he expects answers about his estranged uncle’s mysterious death. Instead, he finds rooms that echo with ghostly whispers, journals filled with warnings, and a fog that seems to know his name.

Drawn deeper into the unsettling legacy of Eldermist Manor, Daniel meets Simon Calloway—a reclusive bookseller whose presence feels like both a balm and a danger. But something in Eldermist is watching. Something ancient. And it has been waiting for Daniel’s return.
