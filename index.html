Fogbound Hearts

When Daniel inherits a crumbling mansion in the forgotten town of Eldermist, he expects dust, maybe ghosts. What he finds is a town drowning in fog and secrets. An abandoned lighthouse. A stranger with eyes like storms. A journal that bleeds memory and madness. As his bond with the mysterious Simon deepens, so does the pull of the ancient curse that haunts the cliffs.

Fogbound Hearts is a haunting tale of queer love, psychological suspense, and a fog that never forgives.
