Inside the Chaos – Weird boyfriends. Wild twists. Zero regrets.

The Worst Boyfriends Ever isn’t just a memoir — it’s a breakup battle cry in bold font and eyeliner. Readers call it “hilarious,” “relatable,” and “the perfect read for a long flight — or a long string of mistakes.”

This isn’t self-help. It’s self-defense, wrapped in poetic rage and brutal punchlines. Every chapter is a red flag parade — ghosters, liars, narcissists, conspiracy theorists — dissected with deadpan honesty and queer fury. One page has you cackling, the next? You’re reevaluating that guy who “wasn’t that bad.”

Told with zero apologies and maximum emotional whiplash, this book isn’t here to fix you. It’s here to fuel you. Because healing sometimes means making a list. Sometimes it means setting it on fire.

You won’t just relate. You’ll feel seen. Then vindicated. Then a little dangerous.
