Queer. Cursed. Kinda Funny.

Not your average book club vibes.

Step into Aleks Filmore’s world of fog-drenched gothic mystery, mythic fantasy, and hilariously awful boyfriends. Whether you crave ancient curses, bear gods, or text messages that should’ve never been sent — there’s a book here waiting to ruin your emotional stability (in a good way).

Fogbound Hearts

When Daniel inherits a crumbling mansion in the forgotten town of Eldermist, he expects dust, maybe ghosts. What he finds is a town drowning in fog and secrets. An abandoned lighthouse. A stranger with eyes like storms. A journal that bleeds memory and madness. As his bond with the mysterious Simon deepens, so does the pull of the ancient curse that haunts the cliffs.

Fogbound Hearts is a haunting tale of queer love, psychological suspense, and a fog that never forgives.

Dacia Rising

A mythic saga forged in the fires of ancient Dacia. As the Roman Empire looms, a young warrior, a high priestess, and a cursed bear must unite the fractured tribes under one banner. Battles rage. Secrets awaken. Destiny roars through the Carpathians. This is more than a fight for freedom—it’s a war for the soul of a forgotten kingdom.

Dacia Rising is a sweeping epic of prophecy, pagan power, and a legacy lost in the mists of time.

Buy on Amazon

The Worst Boyfriends Ever

A hilarious yet heartfelt exploration of modern romance’s biggest flops. From ghosters to gaslighters, dog-obsessed divas to phone-addicted disasters, this collection of dating misadventures will make you laugh, cringe, and maybe even cry a little.

The Worst Boyfriends Ever is your ultimate guide to loving, losing, and laughing through it all.
