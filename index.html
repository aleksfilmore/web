Inside the Chaos

The Worst Boyfriends Ever isn’t just a memoir — it’s a breakup battle cry in bold font and eyeliner. Readers call it “hilarious,” “relatable,” and “the perfect read for a long flight — or a long string of mistakes.”

This isn’t self-help. It’s self-defense, wrapped in poetic rage and brutal punchlines. Every chapter is a red flag parade — ghosters, liars, narcissists, conspiracy theorists — dissected with deadpan honesty and queer fury.

You won’t just relate. You’ll feel seen. Then vindicated. Then a little dangerous.

About Aleks Filmore

Aleks Filmore writes books for the beautifully broken, the hilariously heart-bruised, and anyone who's ever asked “Was it just me?” after a bad date. With a background in storytelling, queer identity, and emotional chaos, his writing blends humor, honesty, and a touch of poetic rage.
