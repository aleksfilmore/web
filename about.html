My Writing Philosophy

Honest, not polite. I write about dating like we actually experience it: messy, confusing, sometimes hilarious, often painful.

Queer experiences, universal feelings. While my stories come from a queer perspective, the feelings of hope, disappointment, and "what the hell was I thinking" translate across all identities.

Boundaries you can actually use. Every disaster taught me something. I share those lessons without the sanctimonious self-help tone.

My goal isn't to make dating perfect: it's to make you feel less alone in the chaos, and maybe laugh at some truly unhinged behavior along the way.
