Cookie Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2025

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They help the site remember preferences, keep you logged in, and gather anonymous usage statistics.

2. Cookies we use

Type Name / example Purpose Expiry
Essential __cf_bm, PHPSESSID Basic security, page loading Session
Preference newsletter_popup_seen Hide sign-up pop-up after you dismiss it 30 days
Analytics *_ga, _gid, _pk_id Count visits and page views (aggregated) 1 day – 2 years

Analytics cookies load only after consent (see §4).

We do not use advertising or cross-site-tracking cookies.

3. Managing cookies

You can delete or block cookies in your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies.
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy.
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security.

Blocking essential cookies may break site functions such as the email-signup form.

4. Consent banner

On your first visit we show a small banner:

“We use cookies to improve your experience. Accept / Cookie Settings”

  • Essential cookies load immediately.
  • Analytics cookies load only after you click Accept.

You can revisit “Cookie Settings” at any time in the footer.

5. Updates

We review this cookie list annually or whenever a new service is added. Changes will appear on this page. Questions? Email aleksfilmore@gmail.com and we’ll be happy to help.
